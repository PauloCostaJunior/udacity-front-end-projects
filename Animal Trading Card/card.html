Arctic Wolf

White Wolf is a mammal of the canidae family and also a subspecies of the Grey Wolf(Canis Lupus).

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus Arctus
  • Average Length: 3 to 6 feets
  • Average Life: 7 to 10 years
  • Habitat: Canadian Arctic and Nothern parts of Greenland

Arctic wolves are also called Polar Wolf or White Wolf. These wolves have small ears which helps the wolf to maitain body heat.
